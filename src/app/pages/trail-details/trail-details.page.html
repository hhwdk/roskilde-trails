Boserup Skov - Kattinge Værk - Boserup Sø

Length: 7,4 km • Est: 2h 10m

Boserup Skov - Kattinge Værk - Boserup Sø is a 7.4 kilometer loop trail located near Roskilde, Zealand, Denmark that features a lake and is rated as moderate.

The trail offers a number of activity options and is accessible year-round. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on a leash.
